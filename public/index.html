Career Profile

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the Jacob Lab at Yale University. I am interested in understanding the molecular underpinnings of plant development and how they can be manipulated for beneficial ends. Previously I have recieved a PhD from The University of Auckland looking at photoperiodic flowering Medicago truncatula. I also have a BA in English.

Teaching

Intermediate R: Data Manipulation and Visualisation

July 2017
Winter Bootcamp 2017
The University of Auckland

This three-hour workshop to post-graduate students introduced the ‘tidyverse’ approach to data science. Targeting individuals who have encountered R but don’t know how to take the next step, this workshop equiped participants with the skills to easily ask questions of their data.

Feb and July 2017
ResBaz and Winter Bootcamp 2017
The University of Auckland

This course formed a third of a two-hour workshop introducing post-graduate participants to the functionality of LaTeX. I discuss the benefits of using LaTeX for large documents and stratagies for approaching such projects. Chiefly it consists of useful functions in producing academic documents.
